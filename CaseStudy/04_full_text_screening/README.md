# Full-text Secondary Screening Records

This folder contains representative input/output examples and batch workflow outputs for the full-text secondary screening step in the radiotherapy and anti-tumor immunity case study.

## Purpose

The full-text secondary screening workflow evaluates records retained after initial title/abstract screening and full-text retrieval. This step links two components of MacAma:

1. **Data Extractor Agent**: extracts structured study information from the full text, including article metadata, animal model details, experimental design, intervention information, detection metrics, immune-response outcomes, and survival-related information.
2. **Secondary Screener Agent**: evaluates the extracted information against predefined PICOS-based eligibility criteria and generates an inclusion or exclusion decision with a reason and rationale.

The purpose of this folder is to provide representative records and batch outputs showing how full-text articles were processed during the secondary screening stage.

## Files

- `secondary_screening_example_input_output_zh.json`  
  Original representative input/output record generated by the deployed workflow.

- `secondary_screening_example_input_output_en.json`  
  English-translated version of the representative example for international readers. The translation preserves the original screening decision, exclusion reason, and reasoning logic.

- `secondary_screening_results.xlsx`  
  Batch secondary screening results generated by the workflow.

## Fields in `secondary_screening_results.xlsx`

- `id`  
  Sequential record identifier used in the case-study workflow.

- `inputURL`  
  PubMed webpage URL of the full-text record evaluated by the workflow.

- `ReExcluded`  
  Output generated when the article was excluded during full-text secondary screening. This field contains the secondary screening conclusion, exclusion reason, and reasoning process.

- `Included`  
  Output generated when the article was retained after full-text secondary screening. This field contains the inclusion decision and the reasoning process supporting inclusion.

- `data`  
  Structured extraction output generated by the Data Extractor Agent. This field records the extracted study information used by the Secondary Screener Agent, such as article information, animal model details, experimental design, intervention characteristics, detection metrics, immune-response outcomes, and survival-related information where available.

## Notes on Language

The complete batch outputs in `secondary_screening_results.xlsx` are preserved in the original workflow-generated language. To facilitate international readability, an English-translated representative example is provided in `secondary_screening_example_input_output_en.json`.
