# Radiotherapy and Anti-tumor Immunity Case Study Inputs

## 1. Research Question

A meta-analysis of animal experimental data on immune activation and immune suppression under low-dose and high-dose ionizing radiation since 2010.

## 2. PICOS Criteria

### Inclusion Criteria

- English-language studies.
- Randomized controlled trials (RCTs).
- In vivo mouse tumor experiments using ionizing radiation as the tumor treatment.
- Studies including at least one irradiated treatment group and one corresponding non-irradiated control group.
- Studies without any concomitant drug treatment.
- Studies published from 2010 onwards.

### Exclusion Criteria

- Meta-analyses.
- Reviews.
- Case studies.
- Studies for which the full text could not be obtained.
- Duplicate reports.
- Studies with very small sample sizes.
- Studies with obvious flaws in study design.
- Studies with incomplete data.
- Studies with unclear outcome effects.
