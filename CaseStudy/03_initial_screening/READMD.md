## Batch Screening Results

The complete batch output of the Initial Screener Agent is provided in:

`initial_screening_results_5598.xlsx`

This file contains 5,598 retrieved PubMed records and the corresponding initial screening outputs generated by the workflow.

### Fields

- `id`: Sequential record identifier.
- `inputURL`: PubMed webpage URL of the retrieved record.
- `PriExcluded`: Agent-generated exclusion analysis. This field contains the exclusion decision, exclusion reason, and reasoning process when the article was excluded during initial screening.
- `PriIncluded`: Agent-generated inclusion analysis. This field contains the inclusion decision and reasoning process when the article was retained for full-text retrieval and secondary screening.

The batch outputs in `PriExcluded` and `PriIncluded` are preserved in the original language generated by the deployed workflow. To facilitate international readability, a representative English-translated input/output example is provided in:

`initial_screening_example_input_output_en.json`

The original Chinese example is also provided in:

`initial_screening_example_input_output_zh.json`
